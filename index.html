My interest in technology: A timeline

4th Grade

I've been interested in computers since I was about 10, when my dad first let me play around with his computer. Ever since then, I've made it a point to keep up with the latest trends and updates in the technological world.

Middle School

I took my first programming class in scratch when I was in 7th grade. It was just drag and drop code, but it set the foundation for the rest of my coding experience.

11th Grade

In 11th Grade, I made my first "real" website in my IB Computer Science class. I made it using a combination of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript that I had learned that year. Scroll farther down to see a screenshot of what it looked like.

i18n.com

When I was 17, in my IB Computer Science class, I created a website that modeled a computer science forum, where students could ask any questions they had related to technology. It was mostly modeled after stackoverflow, a popular forum where users submit questions related to tech.
